About Us

CompanyProfile

BlueBrain Agro and Allied Services Ltd is a corporate entity that delivers high-quality services in the agricultural value chain with a special focus on production, aggregation, and market linkages. Our service areas include but are not limited to activities from the production through processing and marketing of farm produce. In doing this, we provide out-growers financing, extension and advisory services, training and advocacy, Agri-credit and risk management, linkages to finance, insurance and pensions, research, monitoring evaluation and learning, business incubation, and scaling. Blue-Brain Agro and Allied Services Ltd is a creation of corporate professionals in the agricultural value chain, agricultural financing, MSMEs and project management. Their ambition is to deliver quality services that will improve production and quality of produce, add value and prolong produce shelf life, and provide market linkages so that farmers can make profit from their produce.


With extensive knowledge and experience in the Agri sector, we are highly skilled professionals with committed technical know-how. Thus, we can be relied upon to offer quality services necessary to resolve farmers’ challenges to improved production.


Our business provides technical assistance to Nigeria farmers through training, access to credit options, Business incubation, and scaling. We offer bespoke solutions, and tailor programs to enable individual customers and organizations to manage their cost of capital well into the future.


Blue-Brain Agro ensures that our clients have access to quality and affordable products and services, financial advice, training and capacity building, extension and advisory services delivered by a growing network of independent advisors and financial services firms.


We have developed yield improvement training for participating, and start-up farmers most especially our agribusiness advisory at rural community levels. This training is aimed at developing a functional agribusiness value chain in all LGCs to encourage aggregation and offtaking. In this process, jobs are created, and inter-state agricultural trades are encouraged.


Blue-Brain Agro and Allied Services is driven by customer satisfaction with the objective of building long-term partnerships through personal and professional services. From design concept to bespoke finish, Blue-Brain utilizes a friendly and efficient approach to meet customers’ needs. Whether experimental, prototype or scaleup – Blue-Brain Agro and Allied Services is committed to maintaining continuity and consistency and is dedicated to an ongoing investment commitment, promoting leadership in a global and ever-changing environment.
